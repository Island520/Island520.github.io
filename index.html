Biography

I'm Yandao Huang

I am currently a third-year Ph.d. student at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), co-supervised by Prof. Qian Zhang and Prof. Kaishun Wu. Before I join in HKUST, I was a member of IoT Research Center of Shenzhen University . I obtained my bachelor degree in computer science and technology from Shenzhen University, China in July 2020, awarded the highest student honor of "Excellent Star of SZU".

My research interests are highly interdisciplinary, incorporating computer science with advanced material, physics, medicine, physiology, etc. The proposed systems are aiming to be practically used in real-world scenarios.

  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Smart Healthcare
  • Human Activities Recognition
  • Human-computer Interaction
  • Mobile and Ubiquitous Computing
